Three tiers based on funding stage:

- **Tier 3 — $10k**: Bootstrapped or self-funded, under $1M raised. Includes community and events access.
- **Tier 2 — $100k**: $1M+ raised from an affiliated partner. Adds an account manager and technical sessions with Cloudflare experts.
- **Tier 1 — $350k**: $5M+ raised from an affiliated partner. Adds priority technical support and bi-weekly Solutions Architect office hours.

Credits cover usage-based services including Workers, Durable Objects, Workflows, Workers AI (up to $50k), R2 (up to $10k), KV, D1, Queues, Vectorize, Pages, Images, Stream, Cache Reserve, and Argo Smart Routing.

Core security and networking features are included at no cost across all tiers: DDoS and bot protection, WAF with 1,000 custom rules, SSL certificates, CDN and DNS, Load Balancing (2 origins), and a 100% SLA. Each tier also includes three enterprise domains and unlimited Business and Pro plans.
